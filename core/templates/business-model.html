Business Model

In this section we're presenting our proposed revenue and cost model based on the sports arenas examples. In particular, we're showing our estimates for MLB and NFL games.



Revenue Model

Primary Service

For Stadiums/Arenas the revenue model that will be used for our product and services will be a 10% commission collected on VIP/Suite total sales per event. There will be a standard charge for the equipment, such as for the bracelets and nodes. This data will be used to increase profits and revenue at events.

Future

We will work with artists/musicians on tour and provide a subscription service model that fits their needs.The subscription price will be based on the amount of user data that needs to be collected and stored. There will be a standard charge for the equipment, such as for the bracelets and nodes.

Costs Model

Infrastructure Cost

The infrastructure set up cost is motivated by the need to place UWB anchors around the VIP suites in the venue. Our current estimates are calculated using Petco Park in San Diego as the example

MLB Estimates - PETCO PARK
Cost Item Value

cost per module $20
total cost of hardware $42000

The total initial cost of hardware with installation $42000 + $4200 = $46000

Additional cost of maintenance of hardware and server maintenance $46000*10%/year + $4800/year = $9400/year
