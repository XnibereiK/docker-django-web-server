User Interactions

To be able to thoroughly test our system and better illustrate our concepts, we've created a simulation environment that shows the flow of a simple event. Here we have position history for 5 of us, creators, being attendees to the venue. We start by coming in from the left and we're getting checked in by one of the 2 scanners (green) which is where location tracking starts. We then proceed to go to different vendors and make some purchases using a BANGLET system. At the end of the event, our banglets are deactivated and no morre tracking takes place. Simple, seamless, and efficient. For a better VIP experience.
