Event Organizer

Through our UX, we wish to express our focus on the hassle-free experience for end users. Following this philosophy, we want eliminate the common pain points that commonly accompany such events both for the user and the event organizer. Let's talk. How many times did you:

  • not know where to go/ how to get somewhere?
  • have trouble finding somebody?
  • worry about your wallet (credit card, ID, tickets)?
  • want to order something delivered to you directly?
