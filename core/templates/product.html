So... What is Banglet?

You see, venues are often looking for ways to improve the experience of attendees during events. Our solution takes a B2B perspective by targeting festival/concert venues as our customers and aiming to offer them access to VIP attendee location to enable services such as concierge (food and drink delivery). This would be achieved thanks to UWB technology that allows for precise tracking indoors. It can be leveraged to create a platform for attendees to be able to find people and navigate around venues easily.

This could also enable a concierge-type service for VIPs (e.g. order food/drinks to be delivered directly). Friends before attending events can link their bracelets together and recorded information like location tracking will be communicated between phones and bracelets of the friends. Having all the necessary information stored on the bracelet would allow participants to not worry in case the phone battery dies, because payment, identification, and ticket access can be stored in the database for the attendee.

This communication of data allows attendees to get the next-level hassle free and safe experience during the event. Adding custom skins we design will also allow users to feel more individualized at venues and be able to add another sense of fashion to attending events while promoting health and safety. After the concert it would essentially become a souvenir or a collectible item that would have unique designs for each of the festivals.

Empathy Maps

Empathy maps demonstrate our understanding of how our customers (Venues) and users (VIP attendees) will approach the product based on what they feel, thing, say, and do. They help our team establish the main pain points regarding current solutions, the potential risk areas whne it comes to our implementations and specific instances of situations where we can make a difference.
