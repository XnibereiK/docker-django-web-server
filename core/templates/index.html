Bangle Care

Concept Idea

Bangle is a senior care smart bracelet that will help caregivers to monitor location of their older relatives.

Motivation

Doesn't it feel great to know our loved ones are safe and well? Of course, but living separately might not let you react quick enough. In case something happens, you are likely going to find out about it after the fact. Getting notified early can make a tremendous difference. It could give you time necessary to react and prevent a serious injury

Features

  1. Track user location
  2. Ergonomic Design
  3. Easy Setup
