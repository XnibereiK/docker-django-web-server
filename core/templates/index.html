For a Better VIP Experience

Design Concept

Banglet is a smart bracelet product that offers venues access to VIP attendee locations to enable services such as concierge (food and drink delivery). When it comes to end-users, the bracelet could enable touch payments, seamless identity check for access to VIP-restricted areas and ordering drinks, as well as potentially an option to find points and persons of interest (bathrooms, medical team, other bracelet users).

Motivation

Venues often have hundreds to thousands of people attending and it can be tricky for attendees to find the points of interest such as certain stages, food trucks or bars. Even having the online map of the venue it might be difficult to decipher the current location and figure out which way you’re supposed to go and which sections you can access. Another common issue is getting separated from others by accident. GPS and location sharing with friends is simply not accurate enough and doesn’t have the flexibility to include other attendees. Leaving the conversation to get food or a drink is frustrating and so is looking through your phone to find the tickets.

Features

  1. Track user location
    1. Offering the concierge/food delivery service to VIP customers
    2. Integrated with the indoors map (possibly)
    3. Can be used to gather friends together if some are lost or want to regroup
  2. Hands-free experience
    1. ID for purchases
    2. Ticket info for access to restricted sections
    3. Payment info
  3. Skins can be added to bracelets as an accessory to make the bracelet aesthetically interesting
